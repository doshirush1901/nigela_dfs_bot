RAGI DOSA

HEALTH: ★★★★☆ (0.8/1.0) • 20min • 210 kcal
Protein: 6.5g Carbs: 40.0g Fats: 2.0g Parents: 12% | Kids: 13%
INGREDIENTS
  • Ragi flour - 120g
  • Water - 200ml
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Oil - for cooking
METHOD
  1. Mix ragi flour with water and salt to form smooth batter
  2. Rest batter for 10 minutes
  3. Heat tawa, pour batter, cook until edges lift
  4. Flip once and cook until golden
"Sizzle till the edges lift - crispy, golden, and packed with calcium!"

FLAX YOGURT

HEALTH: ★★★★★ (0.9/1.0) • 5min • 120 kcal
Protein: 6.0g Carbs: 8.0g Fats: 7.0g Parents: 7% | Kids: 8%
INGREDIENTS
  • Greek yogurt - 200g
  • Ground flaxseed - 1 tbsp
  • Honey - 1 tsp (optional)
METHOD
  1. Mix flaxseed with yogurt
  2. Let rest for 5 minutes to thicken
  3. Add honey if desired sweetness
  4. Serve chilled in small bowl
